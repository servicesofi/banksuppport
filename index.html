Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our support services.

You can report a lost or stolen card immediately through our mobile app, online banking, or by calling our 24/7 customer service hotline at 1-800-763-4567. We'll block the card to prevent unauthorized transactions and guide you through ordering a replacement.

The fund recovery process typically takes 10 business days for initial investigation. If the claim is approved, funds are usually returned within 3-5 additional business days. Complex cases may take up to 45 days as we work to resolve your claim thoroughly.

Yes, we offer instant virtual cards that you can add to your mobile wallet immediately after reporting your card lost or stolen. This allows you to make purchases while waiting for your physical card to arrive. Physical cards typically arrive within 5-7 business days.

We employ multi-factor authentication, encryption, and continuous monitoring during recovery. Our team follows strict verification protocols, and we'll never ask for sensitive information via email or text. All recovery communications are conducted through secure channels.

We recommend enabling all security features in your account settings, including transaction alerts, biometric login, and two-factor authentication. Regularly monitor your account activity, use strong unique passwords, and never share your credentials. Our security center offers additional tips and tools.
